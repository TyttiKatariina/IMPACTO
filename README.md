<h1>IMPACTO</h1>
IMPACTO represents the conditions under which a Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) is required according to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the European Data Protection Board (EDPB) Guidelines and Data Protection Authorities Guidance from 27 EU member states and 3 European Economic Area (EEA) member states. https://w3id.org/IMPACTO


Cite as: Tytti Rintamäki, Edoardo Celeste and Harshvardhan J. Pandit. “IMPACTO: An Ontology for Representing the Requirements for Conducting an Impact Assessment Based on the GDPR, the EDPB Guidelines and DPA's Guidance." Pending publication
