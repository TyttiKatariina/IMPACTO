IMPACTO (Impact Assessment Ontology) is an ontology for expressing conditions under which a Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) is required according to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the European Data Protection Board (EDPB) Guidelines and Data Protection Authorities Guidance from 27 EU member states and 3 European Economic Area (EEA) member states. IMPACTO assists stakeholders with determining when their personal data processing activities are considered "high-risk" and therefor must conduct a Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) according to the GDPR Article 35.
